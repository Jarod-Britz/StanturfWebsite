Stanturf United FC

Established in 1991, under the umbrella of Mitchell's Plain Football Association (MPLFA) which is part of SAFA Western Cape.

Club Objectives

To foster and promote good relations amongst its members and to form good relations with other clubs, which have the promotion of amateur soccer in South Africa as their objective and is to provide a system of development for players and administration through coaching clinics and seminars.

To encourage and promote football as a lifelong sport and one of the best activities to stay fit and active.

To break into the junior market to further promote development and a pathway for juniors to succeed and have fun.

To build a stronger relationship amongst each other within the communities not only on a football lever but to reunite as a family and put the differences aside as today’s life is all about the youth and the future of our country.
