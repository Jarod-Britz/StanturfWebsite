We are offering a senior tournament that brings together the seniors of Mitchell's Plain like the past old glory days of cup football use to be. Although there is the Coke cup, clubs plays their best 12-15 players in these competitions and a lot of seniors misses out on a lot of chances to showcase their talent. This tournament will also be good as it will be of assistance to Castle league and Vodacom teams as a scouting exercise which could open doors for Mitchell's Plain seniors as it's also a way of getting the seniors in Mitchell's Plain and not only certain clubs but gives all clubs the opportunity to show what they can achieve when given the chance.

We would like to keep the players interested in the game and keep them active after the completion of the season. Hosting this event can unify the club members even more because we all have to work together.
